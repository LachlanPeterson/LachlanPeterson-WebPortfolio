MY BACKGROUND

Study History

I went to High-school at St. Peters Lutheran College, Indooroopilly and graduated in the class of 2013. During my time at school I was interested in Maths (C & B), Physics and Information Technology. Both my parents came from an IT background as Computer Science subjects in university were first emerging, so its no wonder I enjoyed these subjects. I consider myself quite a rationale thinker, and thorougly enjoyed these subjects. Thus, I ended up finishing High-school with an OP-5.

However, when it came to university I didn't continue with mathematics by pursing Engineering, or even IT through a Computer Science Degree. I think that although I was good at these subjects at school, I didn't want my job to revolve around Mathematics espeically. So, I did some research and found that I was interested in Psychology and studied a Bachelor of Psychology Science at the University of Queensland for four years. This allowed me to develop quite strong interpersonal and comunication skills, but again I didnt find motivation to pursue this tragectory and I didnt want to become a Psychologist.

So, how did I end up re-igniting my passion for IT and find myself studying a Diploma of Information Techology at Coder Academy in 2023?

Work History

During school I had a few odd jobs for shorter periods of time here and there. The most notable being at Hoppies Carwash at Toowong roundabout when I was 15. This was an interesting job for someone of my age because it was physical labour and quite tiring cleaning around 300 cars with a team on a busy day, however in the same vein they also payed us extremely well compared to the average 15 year old wage. I worked at hobbies for around 6 months before I had to quit as school and extra-curricular activities prooved too time consuming

Through-out university I worked as a Casual Sales Assistant at a Menswear store. This retail sales job helped me develop excellent communication skills with all members of the wider community, made me efficient in sales and gave me a drive to meet set KPI standards or company targets/budgets. I did all of this while maintaining a high level of customer satisfaction.

After working in Retail Sales for around 6 years I wanted to further develop my Sales skills, but in a field of interest. This lead to me think about my history with IT. I started to search for Recruitment jobs revolving around IT and I become a Cyber Security Recruiter. This re-opened a whole new world of IT for me and I was fascinated learning about Security Analysts, Engineers, Architects, Penetration Testers, Incident Response teams and the work they did. However after some time in Recruitment, I decided that I wanted to be a professional in IT and develop my own skillset, rather than sell someone else's.

This was how I re-ignited my passion for IT and transitioned from Retail Sales, to IT Sales and now to building my own IT skillset in Full Stack Web Development at Coder Academy.
