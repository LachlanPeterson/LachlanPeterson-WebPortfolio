WEDDING

AUSTRALIAN FLAG BEARER

I had never previously attended a wedding before and all of a sudden I was thrust into a cultural experience. My partners sister was getting married and her fiance had asked me to join the Groomsman. Not only that, but I was asked to be the Australian Flag bearer for the ceremony and different cultural practices that came with the territory of a Serbian Wedding. It's safe to say I had a fantastic time partying, drinking and celebrating their marriage, as one of the only Australians in attendance. It was an amazing experience and the Serbian culture was incredible.
