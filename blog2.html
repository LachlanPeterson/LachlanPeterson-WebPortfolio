JAPAN

FIRST INTERNATIONAL TRIP

In 2018, my girlfriend and I decided to have our first international trip together in Japan. We flew from the Gold Coast to the Narita International Airport in Japan before hopping on a train to Shibuya Station. After arriving at Shibuya Station and going through a maze that is the Japanese underground train system, we walked out to see Shibuya Crossing at 11pm. It was a cold, windy night in Japan, but Shibuya was alive, and I remember being amazed at how many people were crossing the road. Every 2 minutes there would be an influx of hundreds of people that seemingly came from nowhere.
