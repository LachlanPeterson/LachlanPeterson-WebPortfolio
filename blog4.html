ITALY

THE RICH CULTURE

In 2019, I was lucky enough to travel to Italy. We visted Florence, Rome and Venice and as you can imagine the culture, tradition and people were outstanding. Italian culture is so rich with arts, architecture, music and great food. This was highlighted when we visited places like St. Peter's Basillica, Vatican City, The Trevi Fountain, The colosseum, and countless art museums. There are many special places in Italy, however the highlight of my trip had to be riding a Gondola through the Canals of Venice and under the Rialto Bridge at Sunset with my Girlfriend. It's hard to imagine a more romantic city in the world.
