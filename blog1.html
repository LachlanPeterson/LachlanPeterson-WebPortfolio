BUDDY

NORTH STRADBROKE ISLAND

This was the first time we took our dog Buddy - a Shih Tzu / Maltese cross - to North Stradbroke Island. The picture was taken on the Gorge Walk off Point Lookout on a beautiful summer day. Buddy loved the beaches around Straddie, and would run in the wind to his hearts content. Him and I would often have a race down the beach, and although he was very small and needed to take around 8 steps for my 1 long stride, he was still faster. When the wind was blowing against him, he looked like the Dragon from 'The Never Ending Story'.
