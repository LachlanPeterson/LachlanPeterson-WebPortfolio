SNOWBOARDING

THE SLOPES OF HAKUBA

I just couldn't stay away from Japan, so in 2020 I went back. But this time it was a completely different dynamic... a Skiing holiday with 12 of my close male friends from High-school. This was the definition of a boys trip, as we stayed a Cabin around Hakuba, hitting the slopes during the day and having a few drinks at night. This was also when I learned Snowboarding for the first time - regrettably without lessons. Although I took a beating from the Japanese slopes of Hakuba on the first day, I was able to pick it up relatively quickly as I used to surf a lot in my Teenage years. By the end of the trip, I was flying down the Mountains.
